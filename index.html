Discover the Flavors of Freshness

At Riverside Farmers Market of Louisiana, we're proud to bring you the finest selection of locally grown, farm-fresh produce and artisanal goods. Our market is a vibrant hub where farmers, artisans, and the community come together to celebrate the abundance of our region.

  1. Farm-to-Table Freshness: Our produce is harvested at the peak of ripeness, ensuring that you get the most delicious and nutrient-rich fruits, vegetables, and herbs straight from the farm to your table.

  2. Diverse Selection: From seasonal delights like juicy watermelons in the summer to hearty sweet potatoes in the fall, our market showcases the ever-changing tapestry of flavors that nature offers throughout the year.

  3. Supporting Local Producers: By shopping at Riverside Farmers Market, you're not just getting top-quality products, you're also supporting local farmers, growers, and artisans who pour their heart and soul into their craft.

  4. Artisanal Excellence: Beyond fresh produce, you'll discover a diverse array of handcrafted goods that highlight the creativity of our local vendors. From freshly baked bread and homemade jams to flavorful kettle corn and artisan cheeses, our market offers something for every palate. As you explore, don't miss the local favorites like Cajun boiled peanuts and dry roasted peanuts, along with local honey, small-batch sauces, and more, all crafted with care and attention to quality.

  5. Community Gathering: Riverside Farmers Market is more than just a place to shop – it's a space where neighbors meet, families bond, and friendships grow. Enjoy the smells and tastes of fresh, on-site cooking, along with family-friendly activities that make every visit a delightful experience. Whether you're exploring the market or connecting with local vendors, there's always something to enjoy.

  6. Sustainability Matters: We're committed to sustainable and environmentally friendly practices. By choosing our market, you're contributing to a healthier planet and a more resilient local food system.

Come and Experience the Difference!

Visit Riverside Farmers Market in Prairieville, Louisiana, and immerse yourself in a world of freshness, flavors, and fun. Whether you're a passionate foodie, a health-conscious shopper, or someone who simply loves the charm of a bustling market, there's something here for everyone.

Friends of RiverSide Farmers Market

Bayou Poppers Kettle Corn

Bayou Poppers serves up freshly popped kettle corn, offering a sweet and salty treat that’s perfect for any occasion. Their delicious, handcrafted popcorn is a crowd favorite at local markets and events.

Heart of Bohemia

Heart of Bohemia serves up refreshing lemonade, flavorful snowballs, and tasty nachos, bringing a perfect mix of cool treats and savory snacks to your market experience. It’s your go-to stop for a delicious and refreshing break.

The Farmers Darlin

The Farmers Darlin – Fresh, locally grown produce. A trusted regular at the Riverside Farmers Market, The Farmers Darlin consistently offers high-quality, seasonal fruits and vegetables straight from the farm to your table.
